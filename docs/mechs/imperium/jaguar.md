## Overview

Named for the fierce predator, the Jaguar embodies speed and ferocity. Its vulkan cannon and blaster combination allows it to tear through lighter foes, while its design prioritizes the swift, lethal strike over defense. In Imperium culture, Jaguars are seen as hunters born for war, and this mech carries that tradition.
