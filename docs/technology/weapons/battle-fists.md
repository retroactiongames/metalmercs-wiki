> *"The shortest distance between victory and defeat is often a single punch."*
>

## Overview

Battle Fists are specialized close-assault weapons employed exclusively by the Starcrest Protectorate.

Unlike conventional manipulators, Battle Fists incorporate reinforced striking surfaces, shock-absorption systems, and integrated maneuvering thrusters designed specifically for mech-to-mech combat. When combined with a mech's jump jets, these systems allow a pilot to convert the momentum of an accelerating assault directly into a devastating impact.

Though often described as melee weapons, Battle Fists function more like short-range kinetic weapons, delivering destructive force comparable to a Vulkan Cannon at point-blank range.

---

## Battlefield Role

Battle Fists are designed to deliver overwhelming damage during close-range assaults.

As a Protectorate mech closes on its target, integrated jump jets and maneuvering thrusters are used to rapidly accelerate the machine across the battlefield. While these systems lack the power necessary for true flight, they allow the mech to skim across terrain at high speed before striking with its reinforced fist.

The resulting impact can inflict damage comparable to a direct hit from a Vulkan Cannon, often shattering armor and crippling lighter opponents in a single blow.

Unlike ranged weapons, Battle Fists require no ammunition and cannot be intercepted by defensive systems. Their effectiveness, however, depends entirely upon a pilot's ability to close the distance. Battle Fist-equipped mechs frequently rely upon terrain, smoke screens, aggressive maneuvering, and coordinated assaults to survive long enough to engage their targets.

For this reason, Battle Fists are strongly associated with Protectorate military doctrine. While other powers generally favor stand-off combat, Protectorate commanders often view close-range engagements as opportunities rather than risks. A successful charge can rapidly collapse an enemy formation and create openings that conventional firepower might struggle to achieve.

## History

Battle Fists originated within the Starcrest Protectorate during the centuries following the Great Restoration.

Protectorate military planners recognized that advances in armor technology were steadily increasing the survivability of front-line mechs. Rather than competing directly in long-range weapons development, engineers pursued an alternative solution: converting a mech's own mass and momentum into a weapon.

The resulting Battle Fist systems combined reinforced impact structures with maneuvering thrusters capable of dramatically increasing striking force during an attack. Though dangerous and difficult to employ effectively, the weapon proved devastating in the hands of skilled pilots.

Over time, Battle Fists became one of the defining symbols of Protectorate warfare. Their continued use reflects the Protectorate's longstanding belief that aggression, courage, and decisive action can overcome technological or numerical disadvantages.

Today, Battle Fists remain exclusive to the Starcrest Protectorate and are regarded as one of the most iconic weapons in the Core.
