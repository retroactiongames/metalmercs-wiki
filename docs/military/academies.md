# Military Academies

The Great Houses maintain extensive military academy systems responsible for training future officers, pilots, logisticians, and battlefield commanders.

Although all major academies teach common subjects such as mech operation, combined-arms doctrine, battlefield communications, logistics, and leadership, each state emphasizes different philosophies.

The Confederate Vanguard Union is widely regarded as producing the most versatile officers. Union cadets receive extensive training in logistics, strategic planning, and combined-arms warfare, resulting in commanders capable of coordinating complex military operations across multiple domains.

The Omnisphere Imperium emphasizes martial excellence and individual achievement. Imperial academies cultivate intense competition among cadets and place great importance on personal mastery, battlefield initiative, and tactical prowess.

The Starcrest Protectorate is known for rigorous physical and psychological conditioning programs intended to produce resilient leaders capable of operating in harsh environments and prolonged campaigns.

The Helios Sovereignty maintains prestigious academies attended by many members of noble and wealthy families. While these institutions produce many capable officers, critics occasionally argue that social status influences advancement opportunities.

Orion Corporate military education emphasizes technology, systems integration, battlefield analytics, and advanced simulation environments. Orion graduates are often highly proficient in operating sophisticated equipment and coordinating technologically complex formations.

Advanced simulators and dedicated training mechs are widely used throughout the Core. These machines allow cadets to participate in realistic combat exercises with minimal risk, often utilizing specialized training weapons and scoring systems similar to large-scale laser tag.
