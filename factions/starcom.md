# StarCom

> *“Civilization survives through communication.”*  
> — StarCom Foundational Doctrine

StarCom is the primary communications, navigation, and interstellar coordination authority of the Core Worlds.

Operating independently of the Great Houses under the protection of the Star Regency, StarCom maintains the infrastructure necessary for large-scale civilization throughout known space.

Its responsibilities include:
- interstellar communications
- navigational routing
- hyperspace telemetry
- merchant traffic coordination
- relay maintenance
- archival preservation
- strategic observation
- deep-space monitoring

No major power within the Core can function effectively without StarCom infrastructure.

As a result, StarCom is widely regarded as one of the most essential institutions in modern civilization.

## Origins

StarCom emerged during the early decades following the Great Restoration.

In the aftermath of the dark age, the Core suffered from:
- fragmented communications
- unstable jump routes
- isolated systems
- collapsed navigation infrastructure
- unreliable interstellar travel

As House Caledon and the returning Vanguard forces reclaimed the Core, it became increasingly clear that no lasting civilization could survive without a unified communications and navigation network.

StarCom was therefore established to restore:
- interstellar coordination
- trade routes
- navigational safety
- long-range communications
- centralized archival systems

Over the centuries, the organization expanded from a reconstruction authority into the vast interstellar institution that exists today.

## Neutrality

StarCom officially maintains strict political neutrality between the Great Houses.

Its infrastructure and services are made available equally throughout the Core under Regency charter.

This neutrality is considered essential to preserving trust in the organization’s communications and navigational systems.

Although political accusations occasionally emerge during periods of heightened tension between the Great Houses, no verified evidence has ever demonstrated systemic StarCom interference in Conclave affairs.

Most mainstream political analysts regard claims of secret StarCom political manipulation as speculative or heavily exaggerated.

## Communications Network

The StarCom Relay Network is among the largest surviving technological systems in known space.

The network consists of:
- hyperspace relay stations
- long-range communications arrays
- navigation buoys
- telemetry platforms
- orbital traffic control systems
- deep-space sensor installations

These systems coordinate civilian, commercial, and military traffic throughout the Core.

In many frontier regions, StarCom relay stations serve as the only stable connection to wider civilization.

The destruction of a major relay corridor is considered a severe strategic emergency capable of destabilizing entire sectors.

## Navigational Authority

StarCom maintains the largest known archive of jump-route data in existence.

Its archives contain:
- navigational calculations
- stellar drift analysis
- gravity mapping
- jump telemetry
- route stabilization records
- frontier exploration surveys

Due to the dangers associated with long-range interstellar travel, accurate navigation data is considered one of the most valuable strategic resources in civilized space.

Unauthorized tampering with StarCom navigational systems is punishable under Conclave law.

## The Historical Directorate

Among StarCom’s most respected divisions is the Historical Directorate.

The Directorate is responsible for:
- archival preservation
- historical reconstruction
- record authentication
- cultural preservation
- restoration-era documentation

Because much of humanity’s earlier history was lost during the Collapse and the dark age, the Directorate frequently works with incomplete or contradictory records.

As a result, many historical subjects remain disputed among scholars.

StarCom officially maintains that preserving surviving records — even fragmentary ones — is essential to preventing civilization from repeating the mistakes of the past.

The Corepedia itself is maintained by the Historical Directorate.

## Frontier Operations

StarCom maintains installations throughout both the Core and the frontier regions beyond it.

These outposts are responsible for:
- maintaining relay systems
- coordinating navigation
- monitoring trade routes
- assisting distressed vessels
- observing frontier instability
- supporting long-range exploration

Because many frontier systems lack stable local governance, StarCom outposts often serve as important neutral infrastructure hubs in otherwise unstable regions.

## Security Forces

Although StarCom is not formally considered a military power, the organization maintains extensive security assets to protect critical infrastructure.

These include:
- relay defense garrisons
- escort fleets
- security mechs
- patrol craft
- rapid response units

StarCom security forces are generally regarded as technologically sophisticated and highly disciplined.

Their stated purpose is defensive rather than territorial.

## Relationship with the Regency

StarCom maintains close institutional ties with House Caledon and the Star Regency.

The Regency relies heavily upon StarCom infrastructure for:
- communications
- diplomacy
- navigational coordination
- intelligence analysis
- interstellar administration

Despite this relationship, StarCom remains legally independent from direct Regency control.

This balance between neutrality and cooperation has allowed the organization to maintain trust across otherwise rival political factions.

## Public Reputation

Within the Core, StarCom is generally viewed as:
- reliable
- stable
- professional
- politically neutral
- technologically essential

In frontier regions, StarCom often represents the most visible surviving institution of wider civilization.

Conspiracy theories concerning hidden StarCom influence occasionally emerge in fringe political circles, particularly during periods of political unrest.

Such claims are generally regarded by mainstream observers as speculative and unsupported by credible evidence.

Even among critics, however, few seriously dispute the organization’s importance to the continued stability of the Core.

## Modern Outlook

As tensions between the Great Houses continue to rise and instability spreads throughout frontier space, StarCom’s role has only grown more important.

Today, the organization occupies a position unlike any other institution in known space:
- politically neutral yet strategically indispensable
- civilian yet deeply influential
- decentralized yet foundational to civilization itself

Many historians believe the survival of the modern Core would be impossible without StarCom.

For this reason, the organization’s guiding principle remains unchanged since the earliest years of the Restoration:

> *“StarCom, To Know the Future.”*
