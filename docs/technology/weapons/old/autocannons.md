# Autocannons

Autocannons are reliable ballistic weapons that fire heavy shells at high velocity. They are common throughout the Core because they are rugged, easy to understand, and devastating when properly supplied.

## Battlefield Use

Strengths: dependable damage, good against armor, simple maintenance. Weaknesses: ammunition dependence, recoil, weight.
