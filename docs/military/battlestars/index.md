# Battlestars

![Battlestar](../../assets/images/battlestars/battlestar-generic-b.png)

## Relics of an Age Lost

Battlestars are the largest warships operating within known space.

Every Battlestar currently in service originated during the age of the Golden Empire.

No modern civilization can build new vessels of comparable scale.

## Strategic Importance

The loss of a Battlestar would be considered a national catastrophe.

Entire military campaigns are planned around their deployment.

A Battlestar frequently serves as:

- fleet flagship
- logistics hub
- mobile fortress
- command center
- invasion platform
