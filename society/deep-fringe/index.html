    The Deep Fringe

    Beyond the settled Core and frontier systems lies the Deep Fringe — isolated regions of space with weak governance, poor communications, and countless dangers.

    The Deep Fringe is home to raiders, isolated colonies, mercenary enclaves, and forgotten relics of earlier ages.
