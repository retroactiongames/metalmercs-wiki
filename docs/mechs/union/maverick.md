## Overview

The Maverick was developed for commanders who demanded independence from supply trains. Its rockets and high energy cannon made it a devastating ambush platform, while its jump jets allowed it to strike from unexpected angles. The name Maverick honors both its battlefield role and the defiant Union spirit of breaking from convention to seize victory.
