## Overview

The Challenger was created during the Union’s dueling trials, where mechs were fielded to prove dominance over rival Houses. Mixing rockets, autoguns, and lasers, it was designed for versatility in single combat as well as battlefield challenges. Though less specialized than some designs, its name and legacy are steeped in Union pride: to challenge is to overcome.
