# Common Misconceptions of the Core

## "The Core controls most of human space."

False.

The Core controls only a portion of known human territory.

## "The Frontier is largely inhabited by pirates."

Partially true.

Pirates exist, but many Frontier worlds possess long histories, stable governments, and unique cultures.

## "The Great Houses are the strongest powers in existence."

Unknown.

No reliable census exists for the regions beyond the Frontier.

## "The Empire and the Core are comparable in size."

False.

The Empire was vastly larger.

## "Humanity is fully mapped."

False.

StarCom continuously discovers and rediscovers settlements, ruins, and navigation routes.
