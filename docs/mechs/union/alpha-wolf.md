## Overview

The Alpha Wolf represents the Union’s doctrine of coordinated pack warfare. Armed with paired autoguns, lasers, and missiles, it was designed to anchor wolfpack formations that overwhelmed isolated foes through relentless firepower. Feared for its ability to tear through both armor and morale, the Alpha Wolf earned its name by always leading the kill.
