    Neutral Worlds

    Several important worlds exist outside the direct control of the Great Houses.

    These worlds often serve as diplomatic, commercial, or strategic hubs.

    Citadel

    Population: 4.2 Billion

    Seat of the Stellar Conclave and home of the Star Regent.

    Citadel serves as the political heart of the Core and hosts representatives from every major House.

    Drake's Landing

    Population: 1.8 Billion

    A major commercial crossroads located along several important trade routes.

    Known for its free ports, massive orbital markets, and strong mercantile traditions.

    Titan Prime

    Population: 2.6 Billion

    One of the largest independent industrial centers in known space.

    Titan Prime's shipyards, refineries, and manufacturing complexes make it a vital economic partner for every House.
