# Modern Era

The modern era is defined by the Five Great Houses, the Stellar Conclave, the Mercenary Guild, StarCom, and the uneasy peace of the Core.

## Overview

The Core is prosperous and powerful, but its confidence masks unresolved problems: ancient technology that cannot be replaced, frontier regions beyond reliable control, and historical truths that remain buried.
