# Deckard Noddack

Deckard Noddack is the current executive leader of House Noddack and one of the most influential figures within the Orion Corporate. Known for his reserved demeanor and intense interest in ancient Imperial technology, Noddack has overseen a significant expansion of Corporate research and defensive operations in recent years.

Born into a prominent scientific and political family within the Corporate, Deckard was educated extensively in engineering, history, and systems analysis. From an early age he developed a strong fascination with the remnants of the Golden Age and the Old Empire and the technologies left behind by earlier civilizations.

Prior to assuming leadership of House Noddack, Deckard participated in multiple archaeological and scientific expeditions involving abandoned Imperial facilities and derelict research installations. Several of these expeditions resulted in important technological recoveries that strengthened the Corporate’s research capabilities.

Under Noddack’s leadership the Orion Corporate has increasingly shifted focus toward technological self-sufficiency, advanced research initiatives, and the restoration of ancient infrastructure. This inward focus has generated growing suspicion among rival Houses, many of whom fear the Corporate may be attempting to monopolize lost technologies from the Imperial era.

Political rivals frequently accuse Noddack of prioritizing technological advancement over diplomacy and regional cooperation. Supporters however argue that the Corporate’s scientific leadership remains essential to maintaining the long-term stability and advancement of civilization within the Core.

Deckard himself remains a highly private figure. Unlike many leaders of the Great Houses, he rarely engages in populist rhetoric or large public spectacles. Most of his public appearances are associated with scientific conferences, technological initiatives, or Corporate security announcements.

Within the Core, Noddack has become strongly associated with the growing competition over ancient Imperial ruins and forgotten technologies. Many observers believe the Orion Corporate under his leadership is positioning itself as the leading authority on pre-collapse scientific knowledge and advanced engineering.

Though admired by many within scientific and academic circles, Deckard Noddack remains a controversial figure among rival powers wary of the Corporate’s growing technological influence.
