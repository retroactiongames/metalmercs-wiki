# Early Human History

The earliest history of humanity is poorly understood by the modern Core. Much survives only as fragments: religious texts, damaged archives, contradictory school traditions, and StarCom reconstructions.

The current dating in the Core dates back to the *Age of Expansion*, which is believed to be the point at which humanity began to occupy various worlds. The exact origin of how this came to be is lost to history, as well as the exact location from which humanity originated. Stories of this era discuss ancient engineers developing jump technology and various empires arising in this period. Much of this time is recorded only through myths and legends.


## Overview

This period covers humanity before the rise of the Golden Empire. It includes the first expansion beyond ancestral space, the creation of early colonies, and the first technologies that made permanent interstellar civilization possible.
