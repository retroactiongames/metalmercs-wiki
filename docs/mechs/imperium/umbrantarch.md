## Overview

The Umbrantarch is a fast, fear-inducing strike mech armed with a heavy blaster and scorcher systems, optimized for repeated hit-and-run assaults. More than a combat platform, the Umbrantarch is regarded as a terror weapon—its silhouette emerging through fire and smoke like an omen. Umbrantarch pilots are said to walk with spirits, feared as much by allies as by enemies.
