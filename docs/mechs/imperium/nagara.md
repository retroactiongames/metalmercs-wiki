## Overview

Built during an era of holy wars, the Nagara combined heavy firepower with symbolic weight. With its HEC and heavy Vulkan cannon, it was designed to break enemy champions and restore order. To this day, the Imperium frames Nagaras as machines of salvation through fire and steel.
