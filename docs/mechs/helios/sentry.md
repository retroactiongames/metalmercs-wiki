## Overview

The Sentry was built for watchful dominance, its long-range high energy cannon and missile banks intended to hold borders in clear view. It lacks mobility, but its very presence is a deterrent, projecting Sovereignty vigilance. Many noble estates still keep a Sentry posted at their gates, a statue of steel as much as a guardian.
