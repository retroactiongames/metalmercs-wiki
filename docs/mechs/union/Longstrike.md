## Overview

An aging missile platform, the Longstrike was originally deployed to provide sustained bombardment from behind the lines. With its paired racks and autogun backups, it could soften enemy positions before an assault. Though slow and vulnerable up close, its simplicity and ease of production have kept it in steady circulation across the Core.
