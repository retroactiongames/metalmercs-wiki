## Overview

The Star Slayer represents the Imperium’s mythic ambition. With heavy energy cannons and blasters, it was designed as a “god-killer,” capable of standing against the mightiest machines of rival Houses. Its name is both boast and prophecy—declaring that no star, no throne, and no rival shines brighter than the Imperium.
