## Overview

A relic of the Imperium’s dueling traditions, the Zephyr was designed for blinding speed, able to outpace heavier mechs with ease. Armed with a blaster and light lasers, it darts in, strikes, and vanishes, leaving foes bewildered. Its name speaks to its nature: swift as wind, impossible to grasp.
