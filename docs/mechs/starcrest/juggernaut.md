## Overview

The second heaviest mech ever fielded in the Core, the Juggernaut was first deployed to break the sieges of the Border Wars, where its unstoppable advance became legend. With layered armor, a crushing fist, and a mix of rockets, lasers, and autoguns, it grinds forward until resistance collapses. Entire fortresses have fallen simply by being in its path.
