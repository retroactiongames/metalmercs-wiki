    Technology Levels

    Tech Level Era Availability
    I Modern Core Production Common
    II Advanced Core Production Uncommon
    III Post-Restoration Era Rare
    IV Restoration Era Legendary

    Most military equipment currently in service belongs to Tech I or Tech II production. Tech III and Tech IV weapons originate from the Great Restoration and are prized for their exceptional engineering, efficiency, and rarity.

    Notes

    • Damage values represent the standard Tech I model.
    • Tech II–IV variants generally improve damage, heat efficiency, range, weight, or reliability.
    • Individual manufacturers may produce specialized variants not represented in this reference.
